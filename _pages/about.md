I am currently an **M.S. by Research** scholar at the **Center for Machine Intelligence and Data Science (C-MInDS), IIT Bombay**, advised by Prof. Biplab Banerjee. My research interests lie in Computer Vision, Domain Generalization, and Generative AI.

Previously, I completed my B.Tech at MNNIT Allahabad and worked as a Graduate Engineer Trainee at Reliance Industries Limited. I have also completed research internships at **Sony Research India** and **Clinical AI Assistance**.

My recent work includes papers accepted at **CVPR 2025** and **NIPS 2025**.
